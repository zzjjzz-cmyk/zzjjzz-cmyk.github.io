P2 Gold – Inside Mirror

A 3D puzzle game developed individually in Unity exploring symmetry between a real world and a mirrored virtual world. The player acts as the only light source in reality, while objects invert their light properties in the mirror dimension. Player would solve puzzles using their knowledge of the rules of the worlds, and find out a path out of the tower they are trapped in.

Key Systems Implemented:

  • Mirror world activation and deactivation logic
  • Event-driven puzzle interaction system
  • Dynamic material switching (light/dark inversion)
  • Camera transition between reality and mirror mode

P1 Gold – The Legend of Zelda Remake

A Unity remake prototype inspired by classic Zelda dungeon gameplay. This project focused on validating core interaction systems and combat feel.

Key Systems Implemented:

  • Player movement and directional attack system
  • Enemy AI behaviors (patrol, chase, attack)
  • Hit detection and damage feedback
  • Rapid iteration based on playtesting feedback
